## Company Announcement

Subject: New process for requesting software tools

Hi everyone,

We are rolling out a simple intake process for new software tools, browser extensions, AI tools, and SaaS trials.

Before starting a trial, signing up with your Balto account, connecting Google/Slack/CRM/GitHub, or uploading Balto or customer data, please submit the software evaluation form.

The form helps us check:

- business need and owner
- security and data handling
- SSO and offboarding support
- customer or personal data use
- AI training terms
- cost and contract needs
- duplicate tools we may already have

This is not meant to slow good ideas down. It is meant to keep us from creating shadow IT, orphaned access, surprise spend, or security issues that are painful to clean up later.

Low-risk requests can move quickly. Higher-risk requests will go through the right review path with IT, Security, Finance, or Legal depending on the tool.

If you already use a tool that has not gone through review, submit it now and note that it is already in use. We will handle that as cleanup, not as a blame exercise.

Thanks for helping keep our tooling useful, secure, and manageable.
